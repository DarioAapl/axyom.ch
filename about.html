About AXYOM

AXYOM AI is a private AI assistant trained directly on your website’s content.

It understands your pages like a human visitor would and answers questions using only verified information from your site. The assistant helps visitors find accurate answers instantly, improving clarity, trust, and user experience.

The Model will be released in early 2026.

Precision

Always responding with high precision by grounding every answer exclusively in the website’s actual content, not assumptions or external data.

Transparency

Fully transparent, clearly basing its answers on specific parts of the website and avoiding any information it cannot confidently verify.

Control

Human-first systems with clear boundaries and intent.
